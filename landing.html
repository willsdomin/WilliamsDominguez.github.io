Covid 19 Project

The COVID-19 pandemic has reshaped the world, necessitating extensive analysis to understand its impact. In this project, we employ SQL, a widely used database query language, to delve into the wealth of COVID-19 data. By structuring and querying datasets from various sources, we aim to identify patterns, track the spread of the virus, and explore its socio-economic consequences. Join us on this data-driven journey as we utilize SQL to unravel the complexities of the pandemic and contribute to the global understanding of COVID-19.

Visualization of Electric Car Production using Tableau

In this project, we dive into the captivating realm of electric cars. Using the powerful data visualization tool, Tableau, we explore the intricate details of this burgeoning industry. Through carefully curated datasets and visually appealing dashboards, we aim to uncover key insights regarding electric car price, range on current brand, and acceleration of speed. Join us on this journey as we navigate through the world of data to shed light on the future of transportation.

Analysis and Visualization of Women's Health Tracker using R

Understanding and monitoring women's health is of paramount importance. In this project, we leverage the capabilities of R, a popular statistical programming language, to analyze and visualize data from a women's health tracker. By extracting meaningful patterns and correlations from the tracker's data, we aim to provide valuable insights into menstrual cycles, fertility trends, and overall well-being. Follow along as we combine data analysis techniques with powerful visualizations to empower women in making informed decisions about their health.
